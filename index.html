Make Your Website Responsive

Problems Solved by Responsive Design

There is no doubt that responsive web design is more important than ever in today’s world. More and more people are using mobile devices to do everything including surfing the web, checking emails, shopping online and so on. Making your website responsive will give them a better experience. Your site will have a fluid and flexible layout that adjusts according to the screen sizes. The users won’t have to pinch and zoom any more to navigate, read an article or click a link. Also they will have a much more familiar experience when they visit your website on desktop, tablet, smartphone, etc. Such consistency will make your users comfortable with using your website and make your website more learnable.

Besides, responsive web design is recommended by Google since 2015, which means it is now a critical factor in improving your website’s search engine ranking. A responsive design will help increase your site’s conversion rates and lead generation. A responsive website will require only one set of code, and one version of your website, which makes it more convenient for you to monitor, report or do web data analysis. Also you will most likely spend less time and effort managing the SEO campaign and site content.
